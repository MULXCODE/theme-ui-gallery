# Alerts

Examples of combining `@theme-ui/components` to create and style alerts.

---

### With Close

Including Theme UI component's `Close` component at the end of the alert.

<LiveCode backgroundColor="soft" containerSx={`sx={{display: 'grid' }}`}>{`<Alert>
  This is a notice <Close ml='auto' mr={-2} />
</Alert>`}</LiveCode>

### Rounded with Badge

Nesting a `Badge` inside of an `Alert`.

<LiveCode backgroundColor="soft" containerSx={`sx={{display: 'grid' }}`}>{`<Alert sx={{ borderRadius: '3' }}>
  <Badge bg="secondary" mr={2} borderRadius="3px">New!</Badge>There's a big deal that ends soon!
</Alert>`}</LiveCode>

### Outline

Adjusting styles to create an outline rather than a filled in `Alert`.

<LiveCode containerSx={`sx={{display: 'grid' }}`}>{`<Alert sx={{ border: '1px solid', borderColor: 'primary', bg: 'background', color: 'primary'}}>
  Verify your account to see additional information
</Alert>`}</LiveCode>
