# Alerts

Examples of combining `@theme-ui/components` to create and style alerts.

### Simple

<LiveCode containerSx={`sx={{display: 'grid' }}`}>{`<Alert>
  This is a notice <Close ml='auto' mr={-2} />
</Alert>`}</LiveCode>

### Full-rounded

<LiveCode containerSx={`sx={{display: 'grid' }}`}>{`<Alert sx={{ borderRadius: 50, py: '1'}}>
  This is a notice <Close ml='auto' mr={-2} />
</Alert>`}</LiveCode>

### Rounded with Badge

<LiveCode containerSx={`sx={{display: 'grid' }}`}>{`<Alert sx={{ borderRadius: '3', py: '1'}}>
  <Badge bg="secondary" mr={2} borderRadius="3px">New!</Badge>There's a big deal that ends soon! <Close ml='auto' mr={-2} />
</Alert>`}</LiveCode>

### Outline

<LiveCode containerSx={`sx={{display: 'grid' }}`}>{`<Alert sx={{ border: '1px solid', borderColor: 'primary', bg: 'background', color: 'primary'}}>
  Verify your account to see additional information
</Alert>`}</LiveCode>
